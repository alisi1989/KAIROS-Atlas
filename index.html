What this is

Each row is one variant dated in one 1000 Genomes population: when selection began (onset, with a 90% interval), how strong (selection coefficient s), the sweep mode, and a reliability grade. Unlike a variant catalogue, the axis is time — the question KAIROS answers that a frequency lookup can't.

Reproduce a row

kairos infer --hap … --map …
  --marker rs4988235 --ne 12500
  --auto-tier --json out.json
Every record carries its Ne, model hashes and KAIROS version.

How to read the bar

The ● is the point onset; the faded bar is its 90% interval; the dotted ┊ is the literature estimate where one exists. A wide bar means an approximate point on a calibrated interval.
